As an owner, I want my clients to be able to place orders online, so that the customer-friendliness of our services increases

As a client, I have to complete an order, so that I can place it online

As a client, I need to fill my online cart with products

As a client, I need to pay my invoice, so that I can complete an online order

As system component, I need to calculate the total amount of the order, so that the invoice can be paid

As system component, I want to pay my order online, so that my invoice is paid

As a system component, I need to process payments on the Ogone-payment platform, so that the payment is secured
